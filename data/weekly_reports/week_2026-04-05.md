| `[Business name] — [specific issue]` | "Pennine Print & Design — [issue]" | **1 positive reply** |
| `[Business name] — [vague hook]` | — | No data |

**Winners:**
1. **First name in subject** — outperforms business name alone (early signal from A/B test)
2. **Specific, concrete issue** — e.g., "flagged 'Not Secure'" not "improve your online presence"
3. **Town name** — adds local legitimacy ("your York salon" not just "your salon")

**Failing pattern to avoid:** Vague subjects like "Quick question about [Business]" generate curiosity but lower trust on cold outreach. Reserve for Day 3 bump follow-ups only.

## 5. WHAT WENT WRONG / LESSONS LEARNED

### 5.1 Wrong Person Problem (Trades Businesses)
- **Affected:** Valley View Garage (lead 15, Todmorden)
- **Root cause:** Google Maps listing email was a workshop/generic inbox, not the owner
- **Lesson:** For trades categories (garages, plumbers, builders, electricians), the website contact page email is more likely to reach the owner than the Maps listing email
- **Fix applied:** `prefer_website_email_over_maps_email` flag for trades category leads

### 5.2 Unsubscribe Signal (Fitness Industry)
- **Affected:** Summit Fitness Studio (lead 19, Otley)
- **Root cause:** Unclear — possibly tone mismatch for a consumer-facing fitness brand
- **Lesson:** Fitness studios and gyms often have young, consumer-brand-oriented owners who may be more hostile to "their website has a problem" framing vs a growth opportunity framing
- **Fix for Week 2:** For fitness/wellness businesses, use a positive framing (AIDA/BAB with "you're already doing well — here's how to amplify") rather than PAS deficit framing

### 5.3 Email Enrichment Blocking 5+ Leads
- **Affected:** Leads 41, 43, 45, 48 (all blocked for no email found)
- **Root cause:** Initial scrape captured Google Maps data only; email enrichment for no-website and low-web-presence businesses is harder
- **Fix:** Prioritise email finding for leads with known websites first; for no-website leads, add phone outreach as fallback channel in Week 2
